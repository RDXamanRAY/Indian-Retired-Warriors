Historical Significance


The SKOT APC enhanced the Indian Army's ability to conduct rapid troop movement and border

patrols. Its wheeled design allowed it to cover longer distances quickly compared to tracked

APCs, making it an effective vehicle for patrolling large border areas and providing internal

security. Its amphibious capability added flexibility, though it was primarily used on land.
