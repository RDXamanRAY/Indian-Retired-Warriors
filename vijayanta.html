Technical Specifications


  • Weight : Approximately 38 tons.

  • Engine : Powered by a Meteor engine, producing 640 horsepower.

  • Speed : Maximum road speed of 48km/h.

  • Armament :
  • Primary :105mm L7 rifled gun, efective aganist both armored vehicles and fortifications.
  • Secondary : 7.62mm coaxial machine gun and a 12.7mm anti-aircraft machine gun.

  • Armor : Comprised of rolled homogeneous armor , providing basic protection aganist small arms and light anti-tank weapons.

  • Range : Operational range of approximately 530 km on-road.

  • Crew : The tank was operated by a crew of four (commander , gunner , loader and driver).

The Vijayanta was a significant step in India's journey towars indigeneous militiary production .

As the first MBT designed and produced in India , it became a symbol of India's defene

autonomy during the Cold War. its service during the 1971 Indo-Pak War understand its value

and cemented its place in Indian military history.
